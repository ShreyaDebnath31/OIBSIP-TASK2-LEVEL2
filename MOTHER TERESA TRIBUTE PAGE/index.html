Legacy

Mother Teresa, an Albanian-Indian nun and missionary, devoted her life to serving the impoverished and destitute. She founded the Missionaries of Charity, a religious congregation providing aid worldwide. Mother Teresa's selflessness and compassion earned her global acclaim, including the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979. Her legacy lives on, inspiring countless acts of kindness and humanitarian work.
